Happy Birthday Ammu ❤
Aaj ka din mere liye sirf tumhara birthday nahi hai... balki uss roshni ka din hai, jisne meri zindagi ko roshan kar diya.

Tumhara hona meri duniya ka sabse khoobsurat hissa hai. Jab bhi tum muskurati ho na, lagta hai duniya ruk gayi hai — aur us muskaan mein main apna sab kuch bhool jata hoon.

Ammu, shayad lafzon mein kabhi nahi keh paun, lekin dil se har din dua karta hoon ki tumhara har din muskurahat se bhara ho.

Tumhara gussa bhi cute lagta hai, aur tumhari baatein mere liye sukoon ban gayi hain.

Main janta hoon zindagi hamesha aasaan nahi hoti, lekin jab tak tum mere saath ho, har mushkil asaan lagti hai.

Tum meri dua ho, mera fakhar ho, aur mera sachcha pyaar ho.

Janamdin mubarak ho meri Ammu ❤
Tumhari zindagi mein har saal nayi khushiyan le kar aaye.

Tum jahan bhi raho, hamesha khush raho... aur yaad rakhna,
koi ek insaan hai jo har pal tumse mohabbat karta hai — bina kisi shart ke.
– Tumhara Hasan
